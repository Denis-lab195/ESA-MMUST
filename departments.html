Electrical Engineering

About Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. This field includes power generation, transmission, and distribution, as well as the development of electrical devices and systems.

Key areas of study include circuit theory, control systems, signal processing, and telecommunications.

Civil Engineering

About Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is concerned with the design, construction, and maintenance of the built environment. This includes infrastructure such as roads, bridges, dams, airports, and water supply systems.

Key areas of study include structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, and environmental engineering.

Mechanical Engineering

About Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering involves the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is one of the broadest engineering disciplines and encompasses the principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science.

Key areas of study include robotics, automotive engineering, aerospace engineering, and energy systems.
